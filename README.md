# FreeIran Sources

This repository contains publicly accessible **evidence and informational resources about the situation in Iran**.

The goal of this website is to provide **clear, factual, and well-organized documentation** that allows readers вЂ” including policymakers, journalists, and members of the public вЂ” to better understand the situation of the Iranian people.

The materials included here may contain:

- videos documenting protests
- photographs and visual evidence
- official reports and documents
- testimonies
- statistics and timelines

All information should aim to be **factual, verifiable, and respectful of the Iranian people**.

# Folder Explanations

## documents/

Contains written materials and official documents.

### documents/reports

Reports from credible organizations.

Examples:

- UN reports  
- Amnesty International reports  
- Human Rights Watch reports  
- investigative journalism reports  

File types may include:

- PDF
- DOC
- TXT
- MD

---

### documents/testimonies

Personal accounts and statements.

Examples:

- testimonies from protest participants  
- statements from victims' families  
- documented witness accounts  

If possible include **source information or publication date**.

---

### documents/legal

Legal materials and policy related content.

Examples:

- international legal analysis  
- legal complaints  
- documentation of human rights violations  

---

# videos/

### videos/protests

# Guidelines for Adding Content

When adding new materials:

1. Choose the **correct folder** based on the content type.

2. Use **clear and descriptive filenames**.

Example:
2022-mahsa-amini-protests-video-01.mp4
un-human-rights-report-2023.pdf


Avoid generic names such as:
video1.mp4
image2.jpg


3. If possible include:

- date  
- location  
- source  

---

# Content Standards

This repository should aim to maintain:

- factual accuracy  
- clarity  
- respectful presentation  
- proper attribution when sources exist  

Avoid:

- misinformation  
- manipulated media  
- unverified claims  

---

# Updating Content

To update or add files:

1. Upload files to the correct folder.
2. Commit the change with a clear message.

Example commit messages:
Add UN Human Rights Report (2023)

or
Add protest videos from October demonstrations

# Purpose of This Project

This project aims to provide **an accessible archive of information about the Iranian people's struggle for freedom, human rights, and democratic governance**.

The goal is to ensure that documentation and evidence remain **visible, organized, and accessible to the public**.
